Temu Tariffs 2026: How Much Extra Will You Pay on Every Order?

Updated May 3, 2026 · 6 min read

TL;DR

Every Temu order now has tariffs. The $800 duty-free exemption for Chinese goods was eliminated. Depending on what you're buying, tariffs add 25-57% to the listed price. A $30 item from Temu actually costs $38-47 after duties.

Why Temu Is More Expensive Now

Until recently, Temu's entire business model relied on shipping cheap Chinese goods directly to US consumers duty-free. Packages under $800 entered the US without any tariffs — a massive loophole that gave Temu a 30-50% price advantage over domestic retailers who paid full duties on their imports.

That loophole is closed. The de minimis exemption for Chinese goods has been eliminated. Now every Temu order, no matter how small, faces import duties.

How Does Temu Handle the Duties?

Temu has started collecting estimated duties at checkout for many orders. Look carefully at your order total — there may be a line item for "import duties" or "tax." If Temu doesn't collect duties at checkout, the shipping carrier (USPS, FedEx, or UPS) will bill you when the package arrives.

⚠️ Watch Out

If Temu doesn't show duties at checkout and your carrier doesn't collect them, you might think you got lucky. You didn't — CBP may send you a bill later, or the carrier may hold your package until duties are paid. There is no legal way to avoid duties on Chinese goods in 2026.

Is Temu Still Worth It?

It depends on the product. For items where Temu's base price is 60%+ cheaper than Amazon, it can still be a good deal even after tariffs. But for products where the price difference is small (20-30% cheaper), Amazon or Walmart is likely better once you factor in duties plus faster delivery.

Quick rule of thumb: If Temu's price is less than half of Amazon's price for the same item, it's probably still cheaper after tariffs. If it's more than half, check Amazon first.

Alternatives to Temu

Amazon: Duties already included in the price. Free 2-day shipping with Prime. Easy returns. Often competitive on price once tariffs are added to Temu.

Walmart: Similar to Amazon — duties are baked in. Free shipping over $35. Growing marketplace with competitive prices.

Non-China platforms: Some sellers on Etsy and specialty sites source from Vietnam, India, or domestic manufacturers. These products may still qualify for the $800 de minimis exemption.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Temu charge tariffs at checkout?

Sometimes. Temu has started including estimated duties for some orders. Check your order total carefully. If duties aren't included, the carrier will collect at delivery.

Can I avoid tariffs on Temu orders?

No. There is no legal way to avoid import duties on Chinese goods. The $800 duty-free exemption has been eliminated for China.

Why are Temu prices going up in 2026?

Temu's prices themselves haven't necessarily increased — but import tariffs now add 25-57% on top of the listed price. Some sellers have also raised prices to partially absorb the duty cost.

Is Temu still cheaper than Amazon after tariffs?

For some products yes, especially when Temu's base price is 60%+ cheaper. For products with a small price difference, Amazon is often better once tariffs are added.

Do tariffs apply to Temu returns?

If you return an item, you can apply for a duty refund with CBP, but the process is complex for small amounts. Most consumers don't bother. Factor this into your purchase decision.
