Is It Still Worth Ordering?

For some products, Chinese platforms can still be cheaper even after tariffs — especially for items with no domestic equivalent. But for mainstream products like clothing and electronics, Amazon and Walmart are often competitive once you add duties to the Temu/Shein price.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Temu include tariffs in the listed price?

Sometimes. Temu has started incorporating estimated duties at checkout for some orders. Check your order total carefully — if duties aren't included, you'll pay the carrier at delivery.

Can I return an item if the tariff makes it too expensive?

Return policies vary by platform. You may get a refund on the product but typically not on duties already paid. Check the platform's policy before ordering.
